**Symptom:** Attempt 2 (killed mid-run) showed real rate ≈ 13 details/min at concurrency 2 → 3,405 details would need ~262 min wall-clock + ~25 min search-walk = ~287 min total. Exceeds the new 180-min budget by ~107 min.

**Hypothesis:** Rate may improve in steady state if Oxylabs warms a connection pool; or may be consistent with the 13/min observation, in which case `budgetExceeded:true` will recur on every Sunday cron run. The cron-wrapper handles this gracefully (`status='warning'`, EXIT=0, Final: emitted, Slack alert fires) — but `cohort-create` Monday morning sees only ~58% of the week's Booli FS listings.

**Recommended owner phase:** Plan 09-02 (Job D — same Oxylabs path, same budget-sizing question) OR a small follow-up plan if Job D doesn't surface the same issue. Concrete fix paths if first cron run confirms 287-min reality:
- Bump `JOB_BUDGET_MS` to 300 min (fits the 300-min cron gap exactly; no concurrency change)
- Bump concurrency 2 → 4 (~145 min wall-clock; Oxylabs Advanced supports 50 jobs/sec so well under cap; deviates from Plan 09-01 hardening lock)
- Both (belt-and-braces)

**Severity:** Operational (not cutover-blocker). Job C with partial completion still feeds `cohort-create` with hundreds of listings; the cohort just has fewer pairs than the theoretical maximum.

### 2. Job D's budget will face the same question

**Symptom:** Job D refreshes ~8,000 active cohort pairs (8-week × ~1,000 pairs/week) every 2 days. At 5 sec/call × concurrency 2 = ~333 min wall-clock — fits NEITHER the 180-min Job C budget nor the 240-min Job D-to-Job-A cron gap (14:00 → 18:00 UTC).

**Recommended owner phase:** Plan 09-02 discuss-phase should address this BEFORE Job D is built. Options:
- Bump concurrency for Job D (3-4) to fit the 240-min slot
- Shift cron slots (e.g., Job D 12:00 → Job A 18:00 → cohort-track 22:00 → 360-min Job D budget)
- Narrow the 8-week refresh window (e.g., 6 weeks → ~6,000 pairs → ~250-min wall-clock)

**Severity:** Discovery — not yet a problem, but will surface during 09-02 wet-run. Pre-emptive thinking now saves a discuss-phase iteration later.

### 3. Cosmetic Final:-JSON `status:success` vs cron-wrapper `status:warning` (carried from 09-01)
