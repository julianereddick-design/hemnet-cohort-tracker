| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-19-01 | Denial of Service (cost) | unbounded Oxylabs spend across a multi-segment batch | mitigate | One `setSpendClient(client)` before the loop = batch-wide DB-atomic ceiling (MAX_OXY_CALLS); CeilingError from any segment OR the re-check pass stops remaining work (D-06). |
